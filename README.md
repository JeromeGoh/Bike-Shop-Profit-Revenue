Project Overview

The client requires a Bike Shop Sales & Profitability Dashboard for the years 2021 and 2022 to provide actionable insights into sales performance, profitability, and rider behaviour across various time periods and categories.

Objectives

The dashboard aims to:
1. Track total riders, profit margins, and revenue across both years.
2. Analyse profitability by hour, season, and rider type.
3. Identify high-performing time slots and sales periods.
4. Support data-driven business decisions for marketing and operations.

Key Requirements

Primary KPIs
- Total Profit and Total Revenue for the current year with YoY comparison.
- Profit Margin and Total Riders as headline metrics.

Secondary KPIs
- Sum of Revenue by Season to identify peak earning periods.
- Hourly Profitability Table to highlight the most profitable times of day.
- Rider Demographics segmented by registered vs casual riders.
- KPI Trend Analysis comparing profit and revenue performance between 2021 and 2022.

Expected Deliverables
1. Interactive Power BI Dashboard with all KPIs visualised.
2. Data model supporting analysis by Year, Rider Type, and Season.
3. Insights summary highlighting performance trends and profitability patterns.

Resources Used: 

- Power BI Desktop for data visualisation and DAX measures.
- Excel/CSV dataset for sales and rider information.
